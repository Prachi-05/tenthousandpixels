Skating away on their boards translates to an epitome of freedom to the young Fly Squad community in Kochi, Kerala.

Theirs is not just a story of chasing dreams but also learning to get back up no matter how many times they skid and fall.

Free Flying documents their journey, their milestones as well as the smiles and pains
along their journey - a little peek into the revolution on a skateboard.
